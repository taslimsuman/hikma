SERVICES

THE SMILE TEST DRIVE

The Smile ‘Test Drive’ is the art of designing, creating and “test driving” a smile before proceeding with the treatment. In just 2 visits one can experience an amazing cosmetic transformation without committing to the actual smile. This ensures one is absolutely sure before proceeding with the treatment and is also an assurance that one will receive the desired result.



FULL MOUTH REJUVENATION

Full Mouth Rejuvenation, The ‘Hollywood Smile’ Undoubtedly, a beautiful smile is a confidence booster, making one look youthful, attractive and positive - and not surprisingly, it is desired by many. At HMC, the ‘Hollywood Smile’ is renamed ‘Full Mouth Rehabilitation’ in order to reflect the expertise and craftsmanship required to create the final outcome.



SAME DAY SMILE SOLUTIONS

Oral function and aesthetics can be immediately restored in cases of severe caries, gum disease or missing teeth. One can now receive dental implants and teeth can be fixed in just one visit. Your final result : a beautiful, bright smile that takes you back to your youth!



CERAMIC VENEERS

In dentistry, a veneer is a thin layer of material placed over a tooth, either to improve the aesthetics or to protect a tooths surface. Veneers can be used to restore a single tooth or multiple teeth that may have been fractured or discolored to create a Hollywood Smile or Full Mouth Rejuvenation service. Veneers may also be used to correct mal-positioned teeth, spaces in between teeth, asymmetry and also short teeth.



COSMETIC WHITENING

Cosmetic whitening White teeth normally indicate a healthy and youthful mouth and smile. But over time, teeth may become discolored due to environmental or medical factors



CERAMIC CROWNS

A crown is a dental restoration that completely caps or encircles a tooth or dental implant. It is made of various materials and is typically bonded to the tooth using dental cement.



IMPLANTS

modern dentistry has eliminated the long process of multiple surgical procedures and long recovery time when receiving an implant HMC offers a convenient dental implant treatment called ‘Same Day Implants’ or the ‘Same Day Smile Solution’.



ROOT CANAL

Root Canal Therapy is performed to save a tooth with a damaged nerve pulp due to decay or trauma.



DENTAL BRACES

Dental Braces are used in orthodontic dentistry to correct crowding, misaligned or rotated teeth and to harmonize the relation between the upper and lower jaw, apart from correcting spaces between teeth and various other flaws of the teeth and jaw. Today, with continual advancement in orthodontic technology, a wide range of corrective options is available. Note : this procedure is available at both the branches.



EXTRACTIONS

A dental extraction is the removal of an entire tooth and is usually performed because decay has made the tooth non-restorable.



DENTAL SURGERIES

Oral Surgeries are any number of medical procedure it includes the diagnosis, surgery and related treatment of diseases, injuries and defects involving both the functional and aesthetic aspects of the hard and soft tissues of the head, mouth, teeth, gums, jaws and neck.



FILLINGS

Fillings,refers to dental material used to restore the function of a natural tooth or an implant in the case of a missing tooth. Most often, Fillings are used in cases of dental cavity or external trauma where the tooth structure has been compromised or partially lost.



INLAYS & ONLAYS

An Inlays and Onlays In dentistry, an Inlay is an indirect restoration (filling) consisting of a solid substance that is fitted to a cavity in a tooth and cemented into place.
