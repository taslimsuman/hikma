Ceramic Onlays
Dental caries can severely damage the tooth structure. Hand crafted ceramic onlays matching the original teeth are recommended to restore the teeth back to their original strength and to avoid future discoloration and deterioration.
Bridge - Fixed
Replacing an old bridge requires perfect matching of the existing teeth in texture, color and shape. A bridge that is perfectly fitted results in increased comfort and easier cleaning which will help prevent halitosis (bad breath)

 

Replacing Old Veneers
Improperly designed and placed veneers can leave you with an awkward and unattractive smile which often times cause bad breath. It is important that the Dental Specialist you are dealing with has the extensive knowledge and expertise to perfectly execute this treatment.

 

Partial Veneers
A delicately placed partial ceramic veneer used to eliminate gaps between teeth. This minor change can have a dramatic impact on your smile.
Laser Gum Peeling
Natural Pigmentation in the mouth can be eliminated using a Laser technique and can help illuminate your smile.

 

The Smile Test Drive
The Smile Test Drive is the temporary placement of teeth that are digitally designed and carefully hand crafted to fit your personality and facial features. This amazing procedure will allow you to literally “test drive” your new smile before proceeding with anything permanent.
