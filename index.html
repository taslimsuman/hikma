THE BEST TREATMENT AT THE PREMIERE AMERICAN DENTAL CLINIC IN ABU DHABI

As the leading dental clinic in Abu Dhabi for over 3 decades, Hikma continues to provide the best services through experienced and certified specialists at our fully-equipped dental center. Our dental clinics are led by a certified consultant prosthodontist, recognised by the American Board of Prosthodontists. We are the only clinic in the emirate who can provide you with good, reliable services based on the tenets and standards of the American Board of Prosthodontists, and only one of two amongst all private sector clinics in the country.

A complete range of the best dental care treatments from our expert in-house dentists

We take pride in being a full-service dentist center, with the best team of good dentists and specialists who are completely dedicated to providing you with unparalleled dental care. Our in-house team of seasoned, good dentists draw from years of experience in their particular fields of specialty, such as orthodontics, prosthodontics, oral and maxillofacial surgery, and other general practices. To strengthen our knowledgebase and improve our services, each dentist undergoes regular in-house training at our clinic to meet the stringent local regulations of the emirate, the UAE, and exceed international and American standards.

As a full-service dental center in Abu Dhabi, patients benefit tremendously by having all their consulting specialists collaborating under one roof. By finding ways to improve their appearance while keeping the longevity of their oral health as your primary goal, our dentists are capable of bringing a good and fitting solution to any dental problem.

Our services include traditional solutions such as braces, crowns, implants, veneers, root canal therapies and teeth whitening treatments. We also provide routine teeth cleaning services and yearly checks.

A good American dental clinic housing the best, most advanced technological solutions today

With our technologically advanced facility, in-house laboratory, and certified team of dentists led by our American-certified prosthodontist, our dental center can confidently deliver world-class results to our clients. Additionally, our range of capabilities allows us to offer solutions that few others are capable to deliver, among them are the following:

  • The Smile Test Drive - provides a digitally-rendered image of your "Hollywood smile" and then an actual temporary in mouth fit to give you a preview of what you can expect after the procedure.
  • Full Mouth Rejuvenation - a highly integrated service that includes a multitude of procedures, including surgeries and prosthesis, to give you a completely new smile.
  • Same Day Smile Solutions - replace missing teeth in a just one day with a procedure that combines dental implants and teeth restoration in the same visit.
